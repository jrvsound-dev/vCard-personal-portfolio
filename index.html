    About me

    Colombian Film Academy and Colombian Film Sound Association honorary member. I started mixing and recording live music fourteen years ago and have been working as a professional sound editor, boom operator and production sound mixer for film and audiovisual media for twelve years.

    If you are looking for someone who can not only keep up with your actors, hide lavalier microphones well, be on time recording dialogue with a boom and doing it all without leaving a shadow, but someone who can deliver you a clean sound as well, I'm your guy!

    OR you may be looking for someone who can give clarity to your dialogues recorded on set, build and synchronize backgrounds, sound effects and create sounds that bring realism and give life to your projects fulfilling the international normative and requirements, I'm your guy too!

    What I do

    • Production Sound Mixer

      Responsible for the difficult and beautiful job of ensuring that dialogue recorded during filming is suitably clear.

    • Boom Operator

      Keep up with your actors, hide lavalier microphones well, be on time recording dialogue with a boom and do it all without leaving a shadow?

    • Sound Designer

      Searching for (and often creating) the perfect set of sounds to complement your film.

    • Post Sound Mixer

      The mixing process brings together all audio elements of a project: dialogue, sound effects, foley and music to create the proper soundscape.
